Recipes

Mom's Famous Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag or box of your favorite pre-packaged cornbread mix
  • Lots of Bower Honey Company honey
  • (Optional) 1 tbsp vegtable oil

Directions

  • Open Cornbread Mix
  • Prepare as directed on packaging
  • Drizzle with honey
  • Enjoy!

Honey Sweetened Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of mixed frozen fruit - Berries or tropical mix preferred
  • Lots of Bower Honey Company honey
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of your favorite yogurt
  • (Optional) 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  • Open frozen fruit
  • Pour into blender
  • Drizzle with honey
  • Add yogurt
  • (Optional) Add vanilla extract
  • Enjoy!

Honey Glazed Carrots

Ingredients

  • A number of larger carrots
  • Lots of Bower Honey Company honey
  • A wee bit of salt and pepper
  • A tiny amount of butter or oil to grease up the baking dish
  • (Optional) A drizzle of balsamic vinegar

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350ish degrees
  • Lightly grease up a baking dish big enough for the carrots to all lay flat on the bottom
  • Add carrots making sure they are all laying flat on the bottom and only one deep
  • Drizzle with honey
  • Add salt and pepper to taste
  • (Optional) Drizzle balsamic
  • Bake until tasty while being sure not to burn them
  • Enjoy!

Honey Boozy Drink

Ingredients

  • Burbon
  • Lemon juice
  • Bower Honey Company honey
  • Ice
  • (Optional) An orange slice or cherry for garnish

Directions

  • Add ice to a highball glass
  • Add a squirt of lemon juice. The more the sour-er-er
  • Add some honey
  • Mix
  • (Optional) Garnish with the previously mentioned orange slice or cherry
  • Enjoy!

Honey Parfait

Ingredients

  • A big ol' scoop of tasty yogurt
  • A scoop of your favorite granola
  • A handful of fresh fruit
  • Bower Honey Company honey

Directions

  • Add yogurt to a highball glass
  • Add granola
  • Add berries
  • Add honey
  • Enjoy!
